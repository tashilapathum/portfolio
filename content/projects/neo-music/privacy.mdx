NeoMusic ("we," "our," or "the app") values your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your data security and explains why NeoMusic is a "zero-data" application.

---

## 1. Data Collection
In accordance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, specifically the principle of **Data Minimization (Article 5)**, NeoMusic is designed to function without collecting any personal data.

* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or IP addresses.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, device identifiers, or any form of behavioral tracking.
* **No Analytics/Crashlytics:** We do not use third-party SDKs (like Firebase or Sentry) to monitor app performance or crashes.

## 2. Information Processing
NeoMusic is a **fully offline** application. All data processing occurs locally on your device:
* **Media Library:** The app requires access to your local storage to index and play your music files. This index is stored locally and is never transmitted.

## 3. Android Permissions
To provide the experience, the app requests the following system permissions:
* **READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:** Required to access and play your music files.

## 4. Your Rights
You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since NeoMusic **does not store any of your data on our servers**, these rights are fulfilled by design:
* **Right to Erasure:** Simply uninstalling the app removes all locally stored app preferences and indices.
* **Right to Object:** You can revoke media permission at any time via your Android System Settings.

## 5. Third-Party Services
NeoMusic does not connect to any third-party services, APIs, or advertising networks. Your data never leaves your device.
